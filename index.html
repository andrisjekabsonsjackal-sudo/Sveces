Candle Care Guide

✨ The First Burn

Let the candle burn for 2-3 hours until the wax pool reaches the edges. This prevents "tunneling" and ensures an even burn.

✂️ Trim the Wick

Always trim the wick to 5mm before every burn. This keeps the flame calm, prevents soot, and extends the life of your candle.

🛡️ Safe Placement

Keep your burning candle away from drafts, pets, and children. Never leave it unattended or burn for more than 4 hours at a time.
